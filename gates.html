Automatic Gates

From intermittent faults to complete no‑power situations—if your gate is playing up, we can diagnose, repair and service it, or replace the motor system if it makes more sense.

Call 0498 524 378 Email photos

Common fixes

  • Gate won’t open/close, or stops part‑way
  • Reversing unexpectedly / safety beam issues
  • Remote range problems
  • Solar not charging / flat batteries
  • Hinges, tracks and alignment issues
Repairs first. If it’s economical to repair, we’ll do that. If a full motor swap is smarter, we’ll explain why.
